# Idea Validation Skill (2026 Edition)

## When to Use This Skill
Use when the user shares a new app, tool, micro-SaaS, AI wrapper, or game idea and wants an honest go/no-go decision before coding.

## Core Principles (Backed by 2025-2026 Data)
- ~45-54% of indie SaaS products make **$0** in their lifetime.
- Median revenue across thousands of products is very low (~$25-$100 MRR); top ~30% drive most earnings.
- First paying customer: Ranges from days (strong validation + audience) to 6+ months (poor distribution).
- Realistic path to $1K MRR: Often 6-12 months for successful cases; many take longer or never reach it.
- Strong validation signals: 8-12% landing page conversion or 5-10 pre-sales commitments.
- AI tools now make validation faster, but willingness-to-pay still rules.

**Summary**

**Quick Validation Plan (Aim for 7-14 Days)**
1. **Landing Page Smoke Test** (2-3 days)
   Build a simple page with strong headline, pricing, and email/waitlist. Drive 200-500 targeted visitors via X, Reddit, Indie Hackers, or niche communities.
   Success benchmark: >= 8-12% conversion to sign-up.

2. **Pre-Sale / Fake Door Test** (3-5 days)
   Offer discounted lifetime deal or paid beta ($19-$49).
   Goal: At least 5-10 paid commitments or very strong intent.

3. **Customer Interviews** (3-7 days)
   Talk to 8-12 people in the target niche. Focus on past behavior ("How do you solve this now?") and real willingness to pay.

**Time to First Earning Estimate** (from today)
- Optimistic (great validation + existing audience): 4-8 weeks
- Realistic (typical solo founder): 3-6 months
- Pessimistic (weak distribution): 6-12+ months

**Realistic Revenue & User Predictions** (if you launch and execute well)
- First 1-3 months after launch:
  Users: 50-500
  Revenue: $0 - $300 MRR
- Year 1 realistic MRR (if you keep iterating + marketing): $200 - $2,000
- Year 1 optimistic MRR (strong product + distribution): $3,000 - $10,000+
- Probability of reaching $1,000 MRR within 12 months: Low / Medium / High
  (Based on ~70% of micro-SaaS staying under $1K/month lifetime)
