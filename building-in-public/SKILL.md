# Building in Public

**When:** Progress post from recent work; pair with `indie-marketing` if they want distribution help.

**Inputs:** `git log`, `git diff --name-only`, and user context (app name, feature, blockers). No git? Ask for commits or a one-paragraph summary of today.

**Voice:** Wins, struggles, learnings, what’s next—not a dry changelog. Conversational and honest. One strong post beats generic volume.

**Shape:** Hook → shipped → insight or hard part → next → CTA.

## Output template

**Build in Public — [App] — [Date or Day X]**

**Single post** (copy-paste):  
[text + emojis + CTA]

**Thread** (default for reach):  
1/ Hook · 2/ Shipped · 3/ Learning or challenge · 4/ Small win or “screenshot of …” · 5/ Next · 6/ Question or reply CTA

**Micro** (optional):  
[shortest one-tweet version]

Screenshot or GIF of the change when possible.
